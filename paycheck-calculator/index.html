How to Use These Calculators

Each state calculator estimates your net take-home pay by applying:

  • 2025 federal income tax brackets and standard deduction
  • Social Security (6.2%) and Medicare (1.45%) FICA taxes
  • The state's own income tax rates, brackets, and deductions
  • Optional 401(k) pre-tax contribution deduction

Results are estimates for informational purposes only and do not constitute tax advice.
