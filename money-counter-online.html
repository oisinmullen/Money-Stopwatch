What is a Money Counter Online?

A money counter online is a free browser-based tool that turns your wage or salary into a live, ticking display showing exactly how much cash you're earning as each second passes. Instead of doing the math yourself — dividing your annual salary by hours, then minutes, then seconds — the counter does it all automatically and animates the result in real time.

You simply enter your hourly rate or annual salary, choose a currency, and press Start. The counter immediately begins accumulating at your personal earnings rate, updating every fraction of a second so the display flows smoothly. It works equally well as a cash clock during a work shift, a motivational tool during a long task, or a simple way to visualize the true value of your time. Use the Pause button for breaks so only actual working time is counted. Everything runs in your browser — no account needed.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a money counter online? +
An online money counter is a free web tool that converts your hourly rate or annual salary into a real-time earnings display. You enter your pay, press Start, and the counter ticks up to the penny — showing exactly how much money you have earned since you started the clock. It's useful for visualizing the value of your time, tracking earnings during a shift, or simply as a motivational tool.
How does the money counter work? +
The counter divides your annual salary by the number of seconds in a year (about 31.6 million) to calculate your per-second earnings rate. It then updates your running total every 50 milliseconds so the display appears smooth and continuous. For hourly workers, it first converts your hourly rate to an annual figure using your hours-per-week setting before calculating the per-second rate.
Is this money counter free? +
Yes — this money counter is completely free to use with no sign-up, no account required, and no download needed. It runs entirely in your browser using JavaScript. There are no premium features or paywalls.
Can I use this as a cash clock? +
Yes. A cash clock and a money counter online are the same concept — a real-time display of your earnings as time passes. Enter your hourly rate, press Start, and use it as a live cash clock during your shift. Press Pause when you take a break so only actual working time accumulates, then hit Start again to resume exactly where you left off.
